ABOUT US

Performance Analyzer is a technical analysis tool for traders, investors, educators, and market enthusiasts that provides visualizations and advisory reports on complex data that were once only comprehensible to professional traders.
We provide a comprehensive analysis for Blue chip companies compared to conventional stock price predictors.
Our analyzer shows the impact of events and public sentiments towards companies along with stock price prediction.
Thus, we provide investors with insight into company practices, culture, legal matters which helps them make informed investment decision.
